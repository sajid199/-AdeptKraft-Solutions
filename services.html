Diversity Sourcing

Building an Inclusive Workforce

Strategic Diversity Recruitment

Diversity Sourcing is the strategic process of identifying, engaging, and attracting candidates from underrepresented or diverse backgrounds to build a more inclusive and equitable workforce. Our approach focuses on ensuring recruitment efforts reach a broad spectrum of talent.

Key Focus Areas

  • Demographics: Gender, ethnicity, race, age, and cultural backgrounds
  • Abilities: Inclusive recruitment for individuals with disabilities
  • Experiences: Veterans, career changers, and non-traditional paths
  • OFCCP Compliance and Equal Opportunity Initiatives
